Structural Dynamics Intern at HRL Laboratoires

Summer 2017: Worked with a small team of three engineers on building novel vibration isolators that utilized negative stiffness structures. During my time there, I helped write Matlab code and mex files to run simulations on the behavior of the isolators. In addition, I designed and built the circuit for the first prototype of the isolator, and wrote the control code for it. I did simple FEA in Inventor to refine the planar spring design utilized in the prototype as well. Once the prototype was built, I was also involved in the testing of the prototype as well as in the design reviews where we discussed the issues with the current design and what to change for the next version.
Skills: Arduino, Soldering, Circuits, Controls, Matlab, C++, Inventor
